§6

Preset pools — time-budget selection

Re-design of the 4 presets as time-budget pools: given a user's available time per dataset, run all models in the pool and pick the best out-of-sample score. Selection is greedy (cross-checked by beam search) to minimise the expected min-of-pool ratio vs PLS over the master cohort. Methodology and per-model evidence: bench/scenarios/preset_audit/AUDIT.md.
